## Leadership Gap

### Attitude and Leadership

Attitude matters! And it matters the most for leaders. Attitude is a mood, and mood is contagious. The mood, an essential element for a positive organizational climate, directly influences team performance, creativity, and retention.

### The Gap

The leadership gap refers to the disconnect between what leaders say they value and how they actually behave. This incongruence erodes trust and creates a toxic organizational culture.

### Bridging the Gap

Leaders who commit to self-awareness, seek honest feedback, and hold themselves to the same standards they expect of others can close the leadership gap and build high-performing, trust-based teams.
